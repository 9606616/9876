Joe Hisaishi is a famouse composer, music director, conductor and pianist. He has made music for over 100 films and solo albums. He started to make music in 1974 and is still making new music. He has partnered with studio ghibli and has become the sound of the studio for all to hear. Hisaishi has won seven japanese academy awards for best music, and many more.
